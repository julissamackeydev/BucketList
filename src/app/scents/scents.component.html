Vanilla in Madagascar: The Smell of French Toastclose

If you've never been to Madagascar, you'll be surprised to know that it's known for more than just cute animals! The majority of the northeast coast is actually the land where vanilla plants are grown. There are tons of local markets where you can buy this vanilla in many forms, and travelers are often surprised to discover how cheap vanilla is here. Vanilla is a sort of Madagascar staple and is sold outside almost any other attraction in the area.
